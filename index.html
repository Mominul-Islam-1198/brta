Instructions for License

To obtain a driving license in Bangladesh, you must follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit your application along with the required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Pass the written and driving tests.
  5. Collect your driving license.
